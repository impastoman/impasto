The short version: Stesura keeps everything on your device. There is no account, no sign-in, and no server that stores your recipes, photos, or activity. We don't track you, run ads, or use analytics.

This Privacy Policy explains how Stesura (the “app”) handles your information. Stesura is provided by [DEVELOPER NAME] (“we”, “us”). By using the app you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on any server. Stesura has no user accounts and requires no name, email, or login to use. All content you create in the app — recipes, flour blends, processes, preferments, bake logs, notes, ratings, and photos — is stored locally on your device only.

Camera and photos

With your permission, Stesura can:

  • Use your device camera to take photos of your bakes;
  • Let you choose photos from your library to attach to a bake (handled by Apple's system photo picker — the app only receives the photos you specifically pick);
  • Save a composed share image back to your photo library when you ask it to.

These photos are stored on your device with your other app data. They are never uploaded to us or anyone else unless you choose to share them.

Sharing recipes

Stesura lets you share a recipe with another person as a link or file. When you do, the recipe's contents (and, optionally, a display name you may set in Settings) are included in that link or file and sent through whatever channel you choose — Messages, Mail, AirDrop, etc. We do not receive, intercept, or store the recipes you share. The optional display name is stored only on your device and is included in a shared recipe solely so the recipient can see who shared it; leave it blank to share anonymously.

Our website

If you open a shared recipe link in a web browser without the app installed, our website (stesura.perfectlyfinewares.com) shows a page inviting you to get the app. Like virtually all websites, our hosting provider may record standard technical request logs (such as IP address and browser type) for security and operational purposes. The website does not use advertising or analytics tracking and sets no tracking cookies.

Third parties

Stesura does not include third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking software. We do not sell or share your information, because we don't have it.

Apple may collect information related to your download and use of the app through the App Store, in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy. That is outside our control.

Data retention and deletion

Because your data lives only on your device, you are in full control of it. Deleting the app removes all of its data from your device. We have no copy of your data to retain or delete.

Children's privacy

Stesura is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If we change how the app handles information, we will update this page and revise the “Last updated” date above. Material changes may also be noted in the app or its App Store release notes.
